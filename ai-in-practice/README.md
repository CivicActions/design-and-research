# AI in practice

## Purpose
Defines how AI is used responsibly and transparently in design work.

## Services lens
AI use assumes client-specific constraints around policy, risk, and approval. All use must be evaluated in partnership with clients and adjusted to contractual and regulatory requirements, in addition to complying with the CivicActions AI policy.

## What belongs here
- AI principles and guardrails
- Governance and decision criteria
- Documented experiments
- Approved patterns and anti-patterns
