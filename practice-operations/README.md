# Practice operations

## Purpose
Describes how the design practice is run, governed, and sustained across multiple clients and engagements.

## Services lens
Practice operations acknowledge the realities of services work, including sold scope, shifting priorities, and shared ownership of outcomes.

## What belongs here
- Design lead guidance
- Intake and prioritization models
- Research operations
- Career growth and role expectations
- Practice health and quality enforcement

## What does not belong here
- How-to delivery steps
- Feedback mechanics
- General collaboration norms
