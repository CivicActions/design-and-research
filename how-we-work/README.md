# How we work

## Purpose
Documents how designers collaborate, give feedback, influence decisions, and work together day to day.

## Services lens
These practices reflect how designers operate within client environments, often without formal authority. They emphasize influence, trust, and clarity across organizational boundaries.

## What belongs here
- Feedback and critique guidance
- Collaboration norms and rituals
- Influence and leadership behaviors
- Decision-making and facilitation practices
- Experiments and archived practices (clearly labeled)

## What does not belong here
- Role definitions or decision rights
- Intake, staffing, or governance systems
- Project delivery methods
