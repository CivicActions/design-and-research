# Foundations

## Purpose
Defines the shared values, principles, quality bars, and maturity expectations that guide all design work.

## Services lens
Foundations establish portable standards that apply across client contexts. How these standards are expressed will vary based on client maturity, scope, and constraints, but the underlying bar remains consistent.

## What belongs here
- Design principles and culture
- UX maturity frameworks
- Quality bars and definitions of “good”
- Active practices that signal what the design practice values

## What does not belong here
- Project-specific guidance
- Step-by-step instructions
- Retired or experimental practices
