# Delivery guides

## Purpose
Provides practical, adaptable guidance for delivering design work on projects.

## Services lens
Delivery guides describe patterns for project work in a services context. Teams should scale and adjust these guides based on engagement type, timeline, and client readiness.

## What belongs here
- Discovery guidance
- Research planning and synthesis
- Service design and validation
- Design QA and handoff practices

## What does not belong here
- Practice governance
- Role responsibilities
- Team norms or values
